About Coffee.QR

Coffee.QR is a revolutionary app developed by the FatMan team that allows coffee lovers to easily order their favorite drinks using QR codes.

With Coffee.QR, you can say goodbye to long queues and waiting times. Simply scan the QR code at your favorite coffee shop, customize your order, and enjoy your drink without any hassle.

Our mission is to provide coffee enthusiasts with a seamless and convenient ordering experience, while also supporting local coffee businesses.

Join us on this coffee journey and experience the future of coffee ordering with Coffee.QR!
